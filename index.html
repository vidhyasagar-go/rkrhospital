Our Services

Complete Dental & Pediatric care under one roof

From your child's first check-up to your family's dental wellness, we deliver evidence-based care with warmth and patience.

General Dentistry

Check-ups, cleanings, fillings, and preventive care for a lifetime of healthy teeth.

Cosmetic & Restorative

Whitening, veneers, crowns, bridges, and smile makeovers tailored to you.

Root Canal & Surgery

Painless root canal therapy, extractions, and minor oral surgical procedures.

Pediatric Consultation

Routine well-baby visits, growth monitoring, and developmental assessments.

Child Illness Care

Diagnosis and treatment of fevers, infections, allergies, and common childhood illnesses.

Vaccinations

Complete immunization schedule following national guidelines, in a child-friendly setting.
